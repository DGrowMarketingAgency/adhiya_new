Medi facials

  • Medifacials are where clinical science meets skin pampering. These treatments go beyond traditional salon facials by using active ingredients and advanced techniques that target specific skin concerns such as pigmentation, aging, acne, and dehydration. At Adhia Aesthetic Clinic, each medifacial is tailored based on your skin’s real-time needs.

  • What sets medi facials apart is their customizable nature. We don’t believe in one-size-fits-all skincare. Whether your skin is stressed, tired, inflamed, or aging prematurely, we create a targeted facial protocol using medical-grade products and technologies.
